Who We Are

A Founder-Led
Energy Transition Platform

Kislay Jha founded Fleak Energy with the vision of building a next-generation renewable infrastructure platform for India. He previously worked in London as a corporate finance and M&A advisor in the energy transition space, with a focus on key European markets including the United Kingdom, Nordics, Germany, France, and Belgium.

Over a four-year period (2022–2025), he advised and closed over €1.5 billion of transactions across asset-level, portfolio, and HoldCo structures — working with leading investors and developers across solar PV, battery energy storage systems (BESS), and onshore wind. In January 2026, he moved back to India to establish Fleak Energy, bringing global transaction experience and a long-term perspective to India’s evolving energy markets.

" Build infrastructure that delivers reliable and scalable clean power and Support India’s transition to a low-carbon and electrified economy"
